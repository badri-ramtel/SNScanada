FAQs

SNS is solely run by the energetic volunteering individuals who are like minded and have a common goal of serving community, and supporting our kids to enrich their skills.

SNS prime goal is to foster our Nepali Culture. We do celebrate our major festivals, Nepali New Year, Dashain, Tihar, Picnic, and Teej. You can celebrate closely with us becoming a member filling our membership forms.

Subscribe to our newsletter to get all the updates of events and newsletters. You can also visit our social media and youtube channels to get latest updates about events.

You are always welcome in the dance practice. You need to download the registration form from our download section and send it to srijansilnepalisamaj@gmail.com.

There are multiple ways to contribute to the community via SNS. We can financially support our events by donating using website or supporting us as a volunteer in any event. You can show your interest for these through our website.
